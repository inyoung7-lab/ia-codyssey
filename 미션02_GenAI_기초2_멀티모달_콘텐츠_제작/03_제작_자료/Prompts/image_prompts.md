# EchoLens Image Prompt Guide

## 1. Master Prompt

모든 Scene에 공통으로 적용하는 이미지 스타일 기준이다.

Create an ultra realistic, cinematic, premium commercial with an Apple-inspired visual style, natural lighting, HDR, shallow depth of field, realistic texture, dynamic travel atmosphere, consistent character, consistent product design, high detail, a 16:9 aspect ratio, and 8K quality.

## 2. Negative Prompt

모든 Scene에 공통으로 적용하는 제외 조건이다.

Ensure no watermark, no unintended text, no unintended logo, no blurry imagery, no low quality, no distortion, no extra fingers, no deformed hands, no duplicate person, no cropped face, no unrealistic anatomy, and no inconsistent product design.

**목적**

**영어 프롬프트**

Create an ultra realistic, cinematic, premium travel commercial from a first-person POV, walking forward through airport automatic doors while holding a passport in one hand as a journey begins, warm natural lighting, HDR, shallow depth of field, dynamic forward movement, high detail, immersive travel atmosphere, no visible smart glasses, no text, a 16:9 aspect ratio, and 8K quality.
